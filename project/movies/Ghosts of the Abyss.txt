==Plot==
Director [[James Cameron]] returns to the site of the 1912 wreck of the [[RMS Titanic|RMS ''Titanic'']], aboard the Russian research vessel ''[[Akademik Mstislav Keldysh]]'' with a team of history and marine experts, and his friend [[Bill Paxton]].<ref name=YMov/> Cameron and the crew document the interiors and exteriors of the wreckage using 3D technology designed for the documentary. While diving on September 11, 2001, the crew hears about the [[September 11 attacks|9/11 attacks]] and they compare it to disaster of the ''Titanic.''
